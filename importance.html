    • The importance of AI

    It could be easier to state what part of our modern society hasn't touched on artificial intelligence ( AI) to show how important it is to our daily lives, business operations and society. Intelligence machines influence almost every facet of our lives to help enhance efficiencies and increase our human capabilities. AI is so entangled with everything we do; it is hard to imagine living without it.

    AI is a central tenet for the disruptive changes of the 4th Industrial Revolution; a revolution that is likely to challenge our ideas on what it means to be a human being and just could be more transformative than any other industrial revolution we've seen before.
